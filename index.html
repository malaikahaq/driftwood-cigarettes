The Origin

Born
From
The
Tide.

"Some smokes taste like a boardroom.
Ours taste like a bonfire."

Driftwood never hired a marketing department. The campaigns were shot by whoever had a camera. The copy was written on cocktail napkins at 2am. The logo was drawn on the back of a surfboard wax wrapper. And somehow, that rawness became the brand.

Fifty years later, nothing's changed. We still blend on the California coast. We still use Haq's original recipe. We still believe the best cigarette you'll ever smoke is the one you light on a beach at sunset, with nowhere to be and no one to answer to.
